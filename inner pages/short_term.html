Short Term Loan

Short term loans through Link Coop is a great option for small businesses or for personal use. There is no lengthy application process, and you can get approved with just a few basic details. And unlike traditional loans which are paid back over time, short-term loans provide quick cash when your cash flow is lacking. So if you need 5000 pesos this week to help with day-to-day expenses, or need some cash in order to pay for repairs on your equipment, Link Coop can help by providing a short-term loan so you can cover these costs immediately."


Qualifications

  • Current member of Link Cooperative.
  • Co-borrower Qualifications
    • Must have a regular source of income
    • Must agree to co-borrow the loan


Requirements

  • Borrower:
    • Updated and latest Pay Slip
    • Valid ID
    • Latest Proof of Billing
    • SSS or TIN Number must be provided


Co-borrower:

Any Two (2) of the following government-issued IDs
  • SSS
  • Drivers License
  • TIN
  • Passport
  • UMID
  • PRC
