Frequently Asked Questions

Link Coop
How to apply for a loan?
  • Become a Link Coop Member
  • Fill out Application Form
  • Get approved.
Where can I submit my loan requirements?
You may upload a scanned copy of your documents in your Link Coop Account. Please make sure that you upload a clear and colored copy of your requirements.
Why can’t I upload my requirements?
It's either the file is too big or your internet connection is slow. For your convenience, please resize the files you are uploading and make sure that you are on a stable internet connection.
How will I know if my loan is approved?
Once your loan application is complete, you will receive an SMS and email notification of your application status within 24 hours. You can also check the status of your application by logging into your Link Coop account.
How can I receive my loan?
For personal loans, you will receive your loan in your bank account. For other loan types, you will receive your loan in our office.
How long before can I apply for a loan renewal?
You may apply for a loan renewal once your previous loan is settled.
